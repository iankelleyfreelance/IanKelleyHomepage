About

I'm a Junior (by hours) studying Astrophysics and Materials Science at the University of Alabama in Huntsville, currently holding a 4.0 GPA and a 2025 National Merit Finalist standing (36 ACT superscore, 1470 PSAT). I graduated Central Magnet Magna Cum Laude, as an AP Scholar with Distinction and an AP Capstone award. Outside of class, I've spent years tutoring math, English, and physics.

Right now my time is split between research and hands-on project leadership: observing sunspots via hydrogen-alpha telescope with Professor Ming Sun at UAH, a machine learning analysis of the Plethodontidae glutinosus complex with Dr. Trevor Chapman at ETSU, and leading UAH's Space Hardware Club Stratosat Team Discovery through a stratospheric balloon launch. Before that, I led Central's branch of Project SunRISE, a NASA/MTSU solar radio telescope program.

On the software side, I work as a Data Analyst / Software Developer with IISTA, and generally write in Python, C, C++, C#, and Java. I'm also a freelance photographer, architectural 3D modeler, and an avid essayist.
